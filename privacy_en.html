Privacy Policy

App: IPcamVision Pro
Developer: Lorenzo May
Contact: lorys.lg@hotmail.it
Last updated: May 23, 2026

Summary

IPcamVision Pro is a local app. We do not collect any personal data, we do not send your data to any server, and we do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or any profiling service. Everything you configure (cameras, credentials, recordings, snapshots) stays exclusively on your Mac.

Data handled by the app

The app handles the following data on your device, all of which is required for it to work:

  • Addresses and configuration of your cameras / NVRs / DVRs (IP or hostname, ports, stream profiles, PTZ brand, options). Stored unencrypted in the app's preferences (UserDefaults), since this is not secret information.
  • ONVIF usernames. Stored as above.
  • ONVIF passwords. Stored in the macOS Keychain, protected by the operating system. Passwords are never written in plain text to preference files or logs, and are never shared with us or any third party.
  • Video recordings and snapshots generated by the user. Saved in the folder you choose. They are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Interface preferences (grid layout, window size, shortcuts). Stored in the app's preferences.

What the app does NOT do

  • Does not collect identifiers (UDID, IDFA, MAC address, e-mail).
  • Does not send telemetry, usage statistics, or crash reports to us or any third party.
  • Does not use analytics SDKs (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel).
  • Does not use advertising SDKs.
  • Does not profile the user in any way.
  • Does not communicate with any server of ours: we do not have one.

Network communications

The app only connects to:

  1. The cameras, NVRs and DVRs that you configure, on your local network or through DDNS / port-forwarding on the public network. These connections use the ONVIF (HTTP/HTTPS) and RTSP protocols with the credentials you entered. Traffic goes directly from your app to the camera, without passing through any server of ours.
  2. Apple's In-App Purchase service, fully managed by the operating system to handle purchases of Pro features. We do not receive any personal information about you: Apple only tells us that a given purchase identifier has been activated.

In-app purchases

Pro features are unlocked through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit). Verification happens locally on your device through Apple's APIs (Transaction.currentEntitlements); we do not keep or receive any information about your Apple ID, your card, or your payment method.

Permissions requested by the app

  • Local network: required by macOS for camera discovery via ONVIF/WS-Discovery and Bonjour on your LAN. You grant it on first use and can revoke it from System Settings → Privacy & Security → Local Network.
  • Access to user-selected files: the app can save recordings and snapshots only into the folder you explicitly select through the system panel. It does not have access to the rest of the disk.

Exporting and backing up configurations

The app lets you export your camera configurations into a .ipcamvisionprobackup file. By default this file does not contain ONVIF passwords (they live only in your Mac's Keychain). If you import a backup file created with previous versions of the app that contain plain-text passwords, the app automatically moves those passwords to the Keychain at first launch. The backup file is still a file you generate and manage: you are responsible for where you keep it.

"Report a bug" feature

From the app you can open a bug report window. When you press "Send", the app does not send anything over the network: it opens your default mail client with a pre-filled email addressed to lorys.lg@hotmail.it. You decide whether to send it or not. The email contains only what you wrote, plus the app version and macOS version (useful debugging information).

Children

The app is intended for a general adult audience. We do not knowingly collect data about minors because we do not collect data about anyone.

Data retention

All data handled by the app stays exclusively on your Mac. You can delete it at any time from the app's "Settings → Reset data" panel, or by uninstalling the app. Deletion includes Keychain entries.

Changes to this policy

Any future changes will be published on this page and inside the app, with the "Last updated" date at the top of the document updated accordingly.

Contact
